Online vs offline wallet split — implication for B&M store model
Online cannibalisation is real but uneven: Online penetration is 15% of total fashion by mid-2025, growing to ~22–25% by FY30 (CareEdge). This means for every ₹100 of apparel wallet at a pin, ₹15–22 is already online — and growing. B&M store potential must discount online leakage. model input
T2/T3 online growth paradox: 55%+ of new e-comm buyers are from T2/T3, but physical value retail is also expanding there (Zudio 200+ stores/yr). Both channels growing simultaneously — net wallet expansion, not pure substitution.
Apparel segment online value: Online apparel at $17.8B in 2024 vs total organised B&M apparel. Women's apparel is the largest online segment. Ethnic wear growing faster online — discovery/influencer-led. Zudio impact
Agentic commerce + hyper-personalisation: AI-driven curation shifting from catalog to individual experience. Virtual try-ons accelerating online conversion. High return rates (~40% in some categories) a key profitability drag — favours physical for impulse/value categories that Zudio targets.
Circular economy emergence: Resale/rental gaining traction, but mainly in T1/premium. Negligible for Zudio's value positioning in near term.

Gen Z ethnic wear behaviour — wallet model inputs
Indo-western fusion is the fastest-growing sub-category: Co-ord sets, short kurtas with jeans, phulkari blazers with denim — Gen Z is not abandoning ethnicity, it's fusing it. Zudio's ethnic-western range is directly in the sweet spot.
Purchase frequency: Gen Z buys ethnic wear 30–40% more frequently than millennials (Myntra/Deloitte data). Combined with ethnic's 67% wardrobe share, this creates high replenishment demand — strong driver of per-store revenue model. model input
Festival + wedding occasion cycles: Ethnic fast fashion is occasion-led. Multiple occasions per year (Navratri, Diwali, Eid, weddings) create seasonal spikes. For wallet modelling, seasonal multiplier on ethnic category is meaningfully higher than western wear.
T2/T3 women ethnic wear: Growing aspirational demand for branded ethnic at affordable prices in smaller cities. Local boutique (unorganised) is being replaced by value-organised players like Zudio. Unorganised-to-organised transition = pure wallet capture for store potential model. Zudio capture
Women's apparel fastest-growing sub-segment: Women's wear growing faster than men's across all categories — ethnic, western, fusion, athleisure. Brands targeting women in T2/T3 cities with trend-forward but affordable products are structurally advantaged.

India-specific structural advantages — input to multiplier calibration
Mid-market is the core growth engine: India's strong growth is primarily in the mid-market segment — growing at 12–17% vs global low single-digits. Zudio operates at the bottom of mid-market / top of value — perfectly positioned at the inflection point. Zudio positioning
India fashion spend allocation: Indian shoppers spend 40% of their fashion wallet on apparel (vs 52% global average) but 44% on jewellery (vs 13% global). Apparel's share of wallet is lower — but growing. Wallet model must reflect this allocation gap. wallet model input
12% online, 88% physical: Myntra CEO confirmed only 12% of India's fashion is bought online (2024). 75M Gen Z fashion buyers, 16M on Myntra. Massive physical store opportunity remains — validates B&M store potential model premise. model validation
Traditional wear is still bigger, but western wear is the fastest growing: On Myntra, traditional wear dominates absolute revenue but western silhouettes are growing faster, especially in Gen Z women. Zudio's mixed ethnic + western portfolio captures both. Zudio strength
Infrastructure is the binding constraint: Limited high-quality T1 malls; T2/T3 infrastructure expanding but lagging consumption growth. Last-mile delivery difficult. This is why B&M physical presence (Zudio's model) beats pure e-comm in emerging cities. model input
9 of top 20 apparel brands are domestic (2023): India-first brands dominating ethnic + value segments. Zudio as a domestic brand with strong cultural fit is better positioned than international entrants. Supports higher capture rate assumption per pin in your model.
